How To Purchase!

In order to purchase, go to the menu bar at the top of the page and click "checkout". There you will be presented with a range of prices based on what you would like the length of your final edit to be. After you make a purchase, a GOOGLE DRIVE link will be sent to you via email and SMS in order for you to upload the footage/videos you would like edited. 48 hours later the final edit will then be uploaded to the same GOOGLE DRIVE folder for you to download.
